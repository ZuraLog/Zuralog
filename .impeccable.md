## Design Context

### Users
Health-conscious individuals who track fitness, sleep, nutrition, and wellness data across multiple devices and platforms. They use the app daily — checking scores, logging activities, reviewing trends, and chatting with an AI health coach. They expect premium quality and clarity from a tool that handles their most personal data.

### Brand Personality
**Grounded. Alive. Confident.**
Zuralog feels like a journal kept by someone who hikes — organic textures, forest greens, and contour-line patterns. Not clinical, not gamified, not cold-tech. Warm, natural, and unmistakably premium.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Organic / Nature-Inspired — topographic contour-line pattern as the brand signature
- **Theme:** Dark-mode primary (Ink Green #141E18 canvas), light mode secondary (Warm White #FAFAF5)
- **References:** The feeling of premium outdoor brands (Allbirds, Patagonia) crossed with Apple Health's data density
- **Anti-references:** Clinical white dashboards, gamified fitness apps with badges and confetti, generic dark-mode-with-neon-accents
- **Signature element:** The brand pattern (topographic contour lines) applied through blend modes on buttons, text fills, card accents, progress bars, and interactive surfaces

### Design Principles
1. **The pattern is the brand.** Every screen should have at least one element that uses the topographic pattern treatment — it's what makes Zuralog instantly recognizable.
2. **Two greens, infinite range.** Sage (#CFE1B9) and Deep Forest (#344E41) are the only brand colors. Everything else is derived. This constraint creates cohesion.
3. **Depth through light, not shadow.** On dark backgrounds, brighter surfaces and borders create hierarchy. Shadows are only used in light mode.
4. **Gentle motion, calm pace.** Expo-out easing, staggered entrances, 600ms entrance animations. This is a wellness app — it breathes.
5. **Accessibility is not optional.** Always-visible nav labels, WCAG contrast ratios, reduced-motion support, semantic markup.
