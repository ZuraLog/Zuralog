## Design Context

### Users
Health-conscious individuals who track fitness, sleep, nutrition, and wellness data across multiple devices and platforms. They use the app daily — checking scores, logging activities, reviewing trends, and chatting with an AI health coach. They expect premium quality and clarity from a tool that handles their most personal data.

### Brand Personality
**Grounded. Alive. Confident.**
Zuralog feels like a journal kept by someone who hikes — organic textures, forest greens, and contour-line patterns. Not clinical, not gamified, not cold-tech. Warm, natural, and unmistakably premium.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Organic / Nature-Inspired — topographic contour-line pattern as the brand signature
- **Theme:** Dark-mode primary (Canvas `#161618`), light mode secondary (Warm White `#F0EEE9`)
- **References:** The feeling of premium outdoor brands (Allbirds, Patagonia) crossed with Apple Health's data density
- **Anti-references:** Clinical white dashboards, gamified fitness apps with badges and confetti, generic dark-mode-with-neon-accents
- **Signature effect:** The topographic pattern drifts slowly on a diagonal on every surface it touches. The motion is not decoration — it is the brand. Stillness is the exception, reserved for content-focused reading surfaces (feature cards).

### Design Principles

1. **The pattern is the brand — and it moves.** Every screen has at least one element using the drifting topographic pattern. The slow diagonal drift is what makes ZuraLog feel alive rather than static. Animation is the default; stillness must be intentional.

2. **Animate highlights, still the background.** Interactive and high-emphasis elements drift: buttons, hero cards, pattern text, FAB, toggles, checkboxes, sliders, empty states, onboarding. Feature card backgrounds stay still so the content inside can breathe and be read clearly.

3. **Two greens, infinite range.** Sage (`#CFE1B9`) and Deep Forest (`#344E41`) are the only brand colors. Everything else is derived. This constraint creates cohesion.

4. **Depth through light, not shadow.** On dark backgrounds, brighter surfaces and borders create hierarchy. Each elevation step is +8 brighter across all RGB channels. Shadows are only used for the FAB (to lift it above scrolling content) and in light mode.

5. **Gentle motion, calm pace.** Expo-out easing, staggered entrances, 20-second pattern drift loops. This is a wellness app — it breathes. Reduced-motion is always respected; animations resolve to static when the system requests it.

6. **Accessibility is not optional.** Always-visible nav labels, WCAG contrast ratios (Text Primary on Canvas = 13.5:1, exceeding AAA), reduced-motion support, 44×44pt minimum touch targets, semantic markup throughout.
