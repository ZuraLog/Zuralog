# ZuraLog — dnyxstudios Style Video Treatment

**Format:** 30-second TikTok/Reels Ad  
**Style Reference:** dnyxstudios (kinetic motion graphics, UI animations, buttery slow-mo, sound-synced interactions)  
**Target Audience:** Fitness-conscious people using multiple apps (18–45)  
**Tone:** Premium, frictionless, intelligent, coach-like

---

## Visual Flow & Timing

### **SCENE 1: THE CONVERGENCE (0–5 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Your health data is everywhere."

**Visual Sequence:**
- Open on a clean black background with soft, minimal lighting
- 10 app icons (Strava, CalAI, Fitbit, Oura, Withings, MyFitnessPal, Sleep Cycle, Apple Health, Google Health Connect, WHOOP) appear floating across the screen in a scattered, natural drift pattern
- Each icon animates in with a subtle scale-up and slight rotation (ease-out cubic)
- Apps orbit slowly around an invisible center point — creating a sense of chaos and fragmentation
- Sound design: Soft, ascending electronic tones as each app appears; subtle ambient hum under the drift

**Transition Moment (4.5–5 seconds):**
- All 10 apps suddenly decelerate and lock into a tighter circular formation
- A soft white glow/pulse emanates from the center
- Apps slide inward in a synchronized "rush" motion (use **Buttery Slomo** to slow this to 8x at 60fps)
- Sound: Single crisp "whoosh" + descending tone as they converge

---

### **SCENE 2: MERGE INTO ONE (5–7 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Into one intelligent hub."

**Visual Sequence:**
- The 10 orbiting apps compress into the center and merge into the **ZuraLog logo/icon**
- Use fragmented or shattering effects as the apps dissolve into the logo
- The ZuraLog icon grows larger and transforms into a **mobile phone frame**
- The phone screen fills the frame smoothly
- Sound: Layered ascending chimes + impact "snap" as the merge completes

**Transition:** The phone is now center screen, ready for the next sequence

---

### **SCENE 3: THE DATA CARDS (7–15 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Now your data makes sense."

**Visual Sequence:**
- Phone screen displays three stacked or side-by-side **data cards** (kinetic card design):
  
  **Card 1 (Kilometers Ran):**
  - Icon: Running shoe or route line
  - Metric: "42 km this week" with an upward arrow (green)
  - Animates in from the left with a subtle bounce easing
  
  **Card 2 (Hours Slept):**
  - Icon: Moon or bed
  - Metric: "8.2 hours average" with upward arrow (green)
  - Animates in from below with a staggered delay
  
  **Card 3 (Calories Burned):**
  - Icon: Flame or zap
  - Metric: "2,450 kcal" with progress bar
  - Animates in from the right with a staggered delay

- Each card appears sequentially with a smooth slide/scale animation
- Sound design: Crisp, minimalist "bloop" or "pop" sound for each card appearing

**Sound Sync Moment (12–13 seconds):**
- The three cards momentarily "glow" or "highlight" together
- Sound: Single ascending chord (suggests completion/harmony)

---

### **SCENE 4: THE AI COACH INSIGHT (13–16 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Meet your AI coach."

**Visual Sequence:**
- The three data cards shrink and move to the top/side of the screen
- A **chat bubble appears** at the bottom (animated from below with a bounce)
- Kinetic typography: The AI's message appears word-by-word with smooth letter animations:

> "Amazing week! You ran 42km, slept 8.2+ hours every night, and burned 2,450 calories. That's a perfect recipe for fat loss — you've lost 1.2 kg. Keep it up."

- Each word fades and scales in slightly (opacity 0 → 1, scale 0.95 → 1.0) over ~100–150ms per word
- The chat bubble has a subtle glow/shadow to make it feel "intelligent" and responsive
- Sound design: Soft, warm synth note under the typing; a gentle "ding" when the message completes

---

### **SCENE 5: THE CORRELATION REVEAL (16–22 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Works with any data you have."

**Visual Sequence:**

**Part A — Zoom In (16–18 seconds):**
- One of the three cards (e.g., "Hours Slept") **zooms in dramatically** to fill most of the screen
- Background becomes blurred/bokeh'd
- The card shows detailed sleep data: sleep stages, REM time, deep sleep percentage, wake times
- Sound: Smooth, ascending "whoosh" with pitch rise as zoom happens

**Part B — Zoom Out (18–22 seconds):**
- Camera pulls back (reverse zoom) in a **Buttery Slomo** effect (4–6x slow-mo)
- As it zooms out, the screen reveals **8–12 additional data points** flowing in from all directions:
  - HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
  - VO2 Max
  - Resting Heart Rate
  - Weight Trend
  - Mood/Energy Score
  - Hydration
  - Steps
  - Active Minutes
  - Blood Glucose (optional)
  - Recovery Score
  
- Each new data point animates in with a slight arc trajectory and scale-up
- They arrange themselves in a beautiful, flowing grid/circular pattern around the original three cards
- The overall composition feels organic, not rigid
- Sound design: Layered ascending tones (one per data point) creating a harmonic swell; final note is a full, resolved chord

**Text Overlay (22–23 seconds):**
- Simple, minimal text appears: **"Works with your apps"**
- Below it, small icons of 3–4 example apps (Strava + CalAI + Fitbit, etc.)

---

### **SCENE 6: GOALS & GAMIFICATION (23–28 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Your coach notices everything."

**Visual Sequence:**

**Part A — The Decline (23–25 seconds):**
- Screen shows **5 metrics in a clean dashboard view**, each with a circular progress indicator or line graph:
  1. Running Consistency (green, 100%)
  2. Sleep Quality (green, 95%)
  3. Calorie Adherence (green, 92%)
  4. Hydration (green, 88%)
  5. Recovery Score (red, 45%) ← **This one is declining**

- The 5th metric (Recovery Score) animates **downward** in red with a downward arrow
- The other four remain stable in green
- Sound: Subtle alarm tone or warning "ping" as the metric turns red

**Part B — The Coach's Response (25–28 seconds):**
- A new **chat bubble appears** with the AI coach's insight:

> "Your recovery is dropping. You're sleeping fine, but you've had 6 tough runs in 8 days without a rest day. Let's dial back intensity tomorrow and focus on mobility work."

- Kinetic typography animates in as before (word-by-word, smooth easing)
- The red metric momentarily "pulses" or "highlights" to draw attention
- Sound: Warm, encouraging synth tone under the message; softer than Scene 4's tone (supportive, not celebratory)

---

### **SCENE 7: THE FLEXIBILITY CLOSE (28–30 seconds)**
**Headline:** "Built for your life, not ours."

**Visual Sequence:**
- All previous screens fade to a **clean, minimal phone mockup** against a gradient background
- The ZuraLog logo appears centered on the phone screen with a subtle glow
- Minimal text below the phone:

> **ZuraLog**  
> *Your health. Unified. Intelligent.*

- **CTA Button** (optional): "Download Now" or "Join Free"

- Sound: Final, satisfying harmonic chord; fade to silence or subtle brand audio signature

## Visual Design Notes

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** ZuraLog brand color (define in `docs/design.md`)
- **Accent (Positive):** Bright green (#10B981 or similar)
- **Accent (Negative/Alert):** Soft red (#EF4444 or similar, not aggressive)
- **Background:** Deep, clean black (#0F172A or #1A1A2E) or soft white depending on brand
- **Neutral:** Grays for text, subtle shadows for depth

### Typography:
- **Headlines/Numbers:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, SF Pro Display)
- **Body/Chat:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Size Hierarchy:** Numbers should feel significant; text should feel conversational

### Easing Functions:
- **Card animations:** Cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) — bounce-like ease-out
- **Convergence:** Cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94) — smooth deceleration
- **Zoom:** Cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) — standard ease-in-out
- **Text animations:** Cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) — snappy ease-out
- **Slow-mo sequences:** Frame interpolation (Buttery Slomo) at 8x, 60fps

## Technical Production Notes

### Tools & Workflow:
1. **After Effects:** Primary software for motion graphics, kinetic typography, and tracking
2. **Buttery Slomo:** For the zoom-out sequence (Scene 5) and convergence (Scene 1–2)
3. **Alight Motion or CapCut:** Quick revisions and template adjustments if needed
4. **Premiere Pro:** Final edit, color grading, sound mixing

### Key Sequences to Outsource:
- **App convergence (Scene 1–2):** Complex 3D camera movement; recommend tracking with depth
- **Kinetic typography (Scenes 4 & 6):** Can use CC: NeuralEngine or custom expressions for word-by-word animation
- **Zoom effects (Scene 5):** Frame interpolation critical; use Buttery Slomo for smoothness
- **Data card animations:** Template-friendly; can batch-create in AE with expressions

### Export Specs:
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (TikTok/Reels standard)
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 @ 60fps (or 1440 x 2560 for 4K upscale)
- **Duration:** 30 seconds ± 1 second
- **Codec:** H.264 (MP4) or ProRes for editing; export as H.265 for social platforms

## Messaging Reinforcement

### Key Takeaways (in order of appearance):
1. **Your data is fragmented.** (Apps floating in chaos)
2. **We unify it.** (Convergence into one app)
3. **We show what it means.** (Three cards telling a story)
4. **Our AI coach explains it.** (Chat bubble with insight)
5. **We work with any data.** (Zoom out to 8+ metrics)
6. **We notice problems & help.** (Red metric + coach's guidance)
7. **This is built for you.** (Close with brand promise)

### Call-to-Action (Optional but Recommended):
- **Text Overlay:** "Join 10K+ people getting healthier" or "Download Free"
- **Link/Button:** App store link or landing page URL

## Variations & Extensions

### 30-Second → 60-Second Version:
- Expand Scene 3 to show more cards (5–6 instead of 3)
- Add a second coach insight moment (Scene 4.5) showing goal progress
- Extend Scene 6 with a second scenario (positive trend this time)

### Platform-Specific Cuts:
- **TikTok:** Standard 9:16, keep text minimal, emphasize motion
- **Instagram Reels:** Same format, but add captions for sound-off viewing
- **YouTube Shorts:** 9:16, can add more text/CTA at end
- **Landing Page Hero:** Loop-friendly 16:9 version (trim to 15 seconds, loop twice)
