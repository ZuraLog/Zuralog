# Today Tab

The Today tab is the home screen of Zuralog. It is the first thing a user sees when they open the app.

Its job is to answer one question: **"How am I doing today?"**

It gives users a quick, at-a-glance summary of their health for the current day. This includes the numbers they care about most — how much they slept, how many steps they've walked, their heart rate, calories burned, and anything else they've been tracking. The data comes from whatever sources the user has connected (wearables, fitness apps, or manual logs), all merged into one unified view.

The tab also surfaces AI-generated insight cards — short, plain-language observations about the user's health patterns. These are not real-time calculations. They are pre-computed once a day by a background job and stored, so the tab loads instantly.

Users can also do quick logging directly from this tab — tapping to record things like water intake, mood, energy level, or a quick workout — without navigating away.

The Today tab is intentionally simple. It is not the place for deep analysis, historical charts, or detailed breakdowns. Those belong in the Data tab and Trends tab. The Today tab is a dashboard: glanceable, personal, and always up to date.
