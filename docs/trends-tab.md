# Trends Tab

The Trends tab surfaces hidden connections between different areas of your health.

Its job is to answer: **"What patterns am I missing?"**

Most people track health metrics in isolation — sleep over here, workouts over there, heart rate somewhere else. The Trends tab looks across all of those metrics together and finds relationships the user would never spot on their own. For example: "Your heart rate variability is 20% higher on days after you get more than 7 hours of sleep" or "Weeks with three or more runs correlate with lower resting heart rate."

These correlations are computed by the system, not entered by the user. The app analyzes weeks and months of data, runs the math, and presents the strongest patterns as cards that the user can explore. Each card shows which two metrics are linked, how strong the connection is, and a plain-language explanation of what it means.

The Trends tab is not real-time. Correlations are pre-computed by a background job and stored, so the tab loads instantly. The patterns update as more data accumulates over time — the longer a user tracks, the more (and more confident) patterns the system can find.

This tab requires patience. It needs at least a week of data before it can show anything meaningful, and the patterns get richer over months. It rewards consistency.

The Trends tab is the "aha moment" generator of Zuralog. The Today tab tells you what happened today. The Data tab shows your history. The Coach tells you what to do. The Trends tab reveals things about your body you did not know.
