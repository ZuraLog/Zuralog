# Progress Tab

The Progress tab is where users track their goals, celebrate their wins, and reflect on their journey.

Its job is to answer: **"Am I making progress toward what matters to me?"**

While the Data tab shows raw numbers and the Trends tab surfaces hidden patterns, the Progress tab is personal and motivational. It is built around the things the user has explicitly said they care about — their goals, their streaks, their milestones.

Goals are the centerpiece. Users set health targets (run three times a week, drink more water, lose five pounds) and the Progress tab tracks how they are doing against those targets in real time. It shows current progress, weekly comparisons, and whether they are on track or falling behind.

Streaks reward consistency. When a user hits their step goal seven days in a row, or logs their mood every morning for a month, the streak counter reflects that effort. Streaks are a lightweight way to build habits without overwhelming the user with complexity.

Achievements mark meaningful milestones — reaching a goal for the first time, maintaining a streak, or hitting a personal best. They give users a sense of accomplishment and a visible record of how far they have come.

The Progress tab also includes a personal journal. Users can write reflections, log how they are feeling, or have a guided conversation with the AI coach to process their thoughts. The journal is private and serves as a qualitative complement to all the quantitative health data in the rest of the app.

This tab is about motivation and self-awareness. It turns health data into a story of personal growth.
