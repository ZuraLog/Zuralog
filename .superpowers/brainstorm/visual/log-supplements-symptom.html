Supplements / Meds — Field Breakdown

  • Your saved list — Built once in settings or the first time you open this screen. Each item has a name, optional dose, and optional time of day (Morning / Evening / Any time). After setup, logging is just ticking boxes — one tap per item.
  • Mark as taken — Tap any item to tick it off for today. Tap again to untick. The AI tracks consistency — "You took Magnesium on 18 of the last 30 days."
  • Add new — Inline "+ Add supplement or medication" at the bottom. Name is required, dose and timing are optional. Saved to your list for future use.
  • Notes — Free text for anything extra. optional
  • Required — Nothing is technically required. If the list is empty, the screen shows an onboarding prompt to add your first item.

Symptom — Field Breakdown

  • Where / what? — Body area chips: Head, Stomach, Back, Chest, Throat, Joints, Muscles, Skin, General. Multi-select. Required. This is the fastest way to get the most useful data point.
  • Type — What kind of symptom: Pain, Ache, Nausea, Fatigue, Bloating, Soreness, Dizziness, Other. optional
  • Severity — 4-level emoji scale: Mild / Moderate / Bad / Severe. Required. Keeps it human — not a clinical 0–10 scale.
  • When did it start? — Timing chips: Just now, This morning, Yesterday, A few days ago. Helps the AI correlate with past logs. optional
  • Notes — Free text to describe it more. optional
  • Required — Body area and severity only. Everything else optional and labelled.
Why symptoms matter for AI: Symptoms are the most powerful correlation data in the app. A headache logged alone is noise. A headache that consistently follows poor sleep, or meals tagged "Alcohol", or high stress days — that's an insight worth surfacing. The more symptoms users log, the smarter the AI gets at finding those patterns.
