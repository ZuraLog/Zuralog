D — Quick Add with Timeline

Big "+1 glass" button is the primary action — one tap done. Also offers +500ml or Custom. Shows a dot timeline of today's logs so you can see when you last drank.

My take: C (Vessel Picker) is the most intuitive for everyday users — you naturally think "I had a glass" not "I had 250ml." Precise users can still customise vessel sizes. But D is the fastest for daily habitual logging. Worth discussing.
