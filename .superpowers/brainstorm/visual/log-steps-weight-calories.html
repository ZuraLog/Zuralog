🔥 Calories

  • Additive — each log adds to the running daily total (unlike weight which replaces)
  • Quick preset buttons: +200, +400, +600, +800 kcal — for rough estimates
  • Custom amount field for precision
  • Shows remaining vs. daily goal

Note on Steps: Most users with Apple Health or Fitbit connected won't need to log steps manually — it syncs automatically.
